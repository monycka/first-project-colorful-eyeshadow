Colorful Eyeshadow

There are many available colors, types, textures, and finishes.


Eyeshadow comes in many different colors and textures. Eyeshadow is a cosmetic that is applied on the eyelids and under the eyes. It is commonly used to make the wearer's eyes stand out or look more attractive.

Eyeshadow is a staple in any make-up bag. It allows us to easily create a multitude of looks, from natural, everyday to all-out glam by building different shades and tones.

Types of eyeshadows:

  • Powder
  • Pigment
  • Liquid
  • Cream

Eyeshadow textures:

  • Matte
  • Satin
  • Mineral
  • Shimmer
  • Iridescent
  • Duochrome
  • Metallic
